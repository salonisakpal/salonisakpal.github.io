About Me

Meet Saloni, a young girl from Mumbai, India who had always been passionate about dancing since she was a child. She spent most of her time grooving to Bollywood tunes and practicing various dance styles, such as Kathak and Contemporary. As she grew older, Saloni's parents encouraged her to pursue a career in engineering and complete her diploma, which she did with flying colors.
